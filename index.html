Behind the Scenes

Well, what exactly does the student council do and how do they contribute to the school?

Carefully listen to and act on your voices

With our feedback system and through identifying problems on campus in our weekly meetings, we effectively problem solve to help improve the general school environment appropriately.

Communicate ideas with school authorities

If you are the voice, then we are the microphone that makes sure you get heard. Through writing proposals to the school authorities, we can effectively make change to bring great ideas to the school.

And of course, yes we host fun events

The school isn't just a place to learn. It is also a second home, one where you can make connections and take a break. Sometimes, we need some time to have fun, and knowing that, we bring this fun to you.
